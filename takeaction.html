How to Take Action

There are many ways you can take action against climate change. Here are some examples:

  • Walk/Bike instead of Driving
  • Buy sustainable
  • Eliminate food waste
  • Use less plastic
  • Be involved
  • Contact politicians to support climate legislation
  • 3 Rs: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
